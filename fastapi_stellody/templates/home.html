How Stellody Helps You Organize Music

Generate Genre-Sorted Playlists

Stellody is a play on words: Stellar + Melody.

Start with a local music folder or your Spotify playlists.
Stellody analyses your artists, discovers related artists and identifies accurate music genres using MusicBrainz metadata.
It then generates ready-made, genre-based Spotify playlists automatically (Spotify Free or Premium).

Supported audio formats

Type Extensions Notes
Lossless flac alac wav aiff aif aifc wv ape tak WV = WavPack; APE = Monkey’s Audio; TAK = Tom’s lossless Audio Kompressor
Lossy mp3 m4a aac ogg opus wma webm
